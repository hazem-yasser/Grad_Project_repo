C++ Bit-Exact Model Logic

1. Input Latch
Grabs 10 samples (5 Real, 5 Imag) [Re-2...Re+2, Im-2...Im+2]
↓
2. DSP MAC Unit (Per Neuron)
Multiplies inputs by ROM weights Acc = Σ(Ini × Wi)
↓
3. Scaling & Bias
Shift right 14 bits, add bias Acc = (Acc >> 14) + Bias
↓
4. Activation & Clamping
Apply ReLU and clamp to 16-bit Out = Clip(Acc, -32768, 32767)
↓
5. Sequential Pipeline
L1 Results → L2 Inputs
L2 Results → L3 Inputs
↓
6. Output
Write 16-bit Integer
(I_out, Q_out)
